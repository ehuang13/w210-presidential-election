About Us

Discover a New Perspective

Providentia enables campaign strategists and political analysts to uncover election insights beyond polls, identify swing state dynamics, and devise appropriate campaign action plans.


Starting with the 2020 Presidential election, Providentia leverages a multitude of structured and unstructured data sources to provide a differentiated and actionable lens into election forecasting.

Non-polls based election forecasting

We offer a different approach to capture undecided voters' hidden voting preferences

Undecided voters vote by topic

In close-call scenarios, we believe undecided voters vote based on candidates' position on key issues, and not based on ideological party lines.

Cost-effective approach to election forecasting

Our model calculates and pulls in voter sentiments for each candidate, by key issues, monthly.

Latest Model Predictions

DATASET

Publically available democraphic and economic data from the Census Bureau, as well as historical election results from MIT's election lab.

SIGNALS

480,000 tweets, news headlines, and google trends as signals for undecided voter's sentiment on key issues. We also factored in historic bellwether counties and voter participation rates.

PIPELINE & ARCHITECTURE

Our data and model predictions are hosted on Google Cloud Platform, and our visualizations are deployed with Flask and Bokeh.

METHODOLOGY

We trained our model on more than 18,000 county results from the last 6 election years. We also leveraged NLP to calculate sentiment scores for candidates by key issues.
